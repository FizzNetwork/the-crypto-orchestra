Core Laws Discovered (Selected)
Law 1 — Hierarchy
Dominant leader required as base
The signal requires a dominant market leader as base. BTC for crypto, SPY for equities. Without a genuine base asset, the chord framework degrades to noise.
Evidence: Cross-pairs (ETH/SOL, SOL/DOT) all 33–50%
Law 2 — Asymmetry
SUSPENDED is informative; DOMINANT is not
Upward divergence (alt holds while base falls) is genuinely predictive. Downward divergence (alt falls while base rises) does NOT invert — it's structurally different information.
Evidence: DOMINANT reversed gives 44–50% TRITONE prediction
Law 3 — Beta Amplification
Edge scales with alt asset's beta to base
SOL (highest beta) → +7.1% avg. TLT (low beta) → +1.2% avg. The chord framework is a volatility amplifier — position size and asset selection should account for beta.
Evidence: Across all 25+ assets tested
Law 4 — Dying Asset
Secular decline assets resolve SUSPENDED → TRITONE
Assets in structural downtrend (ADA, DOT) use SUSPENDED as a brief pause before continued collapse. SUSPENDED is only a recovery signal for assets with an active bid.
Evidence: ADA 0%, DOT 0%, avg −17%
Law 5 — Bear Regime Edge
SUSPENDED in bear regime is more predictive than in bull
Counter-intuitively, SOL price below 20-week SMA gives 78% MAJOR rate vs 33% in bull. When SOL is already cheap, a genuine divergence is more likely to resolve upward.
Evidence: Arc 8 feature importance analysis
Law 6 — Phase Zero
Filter fires at orbit phase ≈ 0° (zero crossing)
The Hilbert phase of the bandpass-filtered SOL−BTC spread at filter signal times is ≈ 0° (6/7 cases within ±38°). The signal catches the exact moment SOL begins leading BTC, before the divergence is obvious in price.
Evidence: Arc 15 phase estimator analysis
Law 7 — Orbit Identity
The BTC/SOL relationship IS the 4D orbit
Bitcoin alone is not a 1D projection of a lunar orbit (R²=0.5%). But the SOL−BTC spread autocorrelates at τ=4w (lunar period) and SOL alone at τ=5w (=TAU). The chord framework is already the back-map.
Evidence: Arc 14 back-map analysis
Law 8 — Macro Convergence
3+ macro assets crossing simultaneously = highest conviction
Macro assets are genuinely independent (r=0.04–0.17). When 4–5 cross in the same week as BTC weekly crossing, it is a true multi-factor global risk-on signal. 4–6 such events per year.
Evidence: Extended orchestra convergence analysis
Law 32 (candidate) — Macro Convergence Amplifier
Crypto convergence is noise; macro convergence is signal
Crypto 3+ chain convergence happens in 57% of all weeks (same as SUSPENDED filter overlap) — BTC coupling makes it uninformative. Macro assets (Gold, SPY, VIX, Crude, Bonds) are genuinely independent and their convergence events are structurally different from SUSPENDED filter events (zero historical overlap). The SUSPENDED filter + macro convergence simultaneously would be a max-conviction combo. Expected frequency: ≤1×/year. Requires live macro data to validate prospectively.
Evidence: Arc 16 convergence backtest — n=7 filter signals, 4/7=57% crypto overlap = exact baseline
