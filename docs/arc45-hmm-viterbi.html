The W8 signal's historical wins are dominated by BULL-regime SUSPENDED events: the chord fires when BTC temporarily dips in a still-healthy bull run, and SOL bounces back strongly. TRANSIT-regime SUSPENDED is a warning sign — the market is rotating toward BEAR, and SOL relative strength is temporary. Regime filter beats Jupiter gate as a signal discriminant.
