Arc 44 Summary: Jupiter proximity changes chord dynamics in two key ways. (1) Bull runs sustain — MAJOR→TRITONE drops from 46% to 21% inside the window; uptrends are stickier. (2) Risk chords resolve fast — DOMINANT→TRITONE spikes to 80%, and SUSPENDED doesn't repeat (0% vs 25%). The market near Jupiter opposition tends toward clean, decisive moves rather than grinding persistence. The W8 SUSPENDED signal's raw SOL direction edge does NOT improve inside the window on a 1-week horizon (37.5% vs 62.5% outside), but the broader market context shifts significantly.
