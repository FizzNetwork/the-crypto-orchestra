What we now know

The asymmetry law: divergence up signals recovery, divergence down signals nothing

When a high-beta asset holds up while its base falls (SUSPENDED), that's a genuine recovery signal. But when the base rises while the alt falls (DOMINANT), the next move is random — the base simply drags the alt up in the following week. The market doesn't telegraph drops the same way it telegraphs recoveries. Recoveries are "coordinated" events that build from divergence. Drops are reflexive and don't show pre-signals in the chord framework.

Hierarchy law: the signal requires a dominant leader as the base

BTC/SOL works because BTC is the crypto market anchor. SPY/AMD works because SPY is the equity anchor. ETH/SOL, SOL/DOT — peer relationships — don't produce clean signals because neither has clear causal primacy. Transfer entropy flows from BTC to everything else. The signal lives in leader→follower pairs, not lateral pairs.

AMD and NVDA are the "SOL of equities"

The highest-beta semiconductor stocks diverge from SPY in the same way SOL diverges from BTC. When SPY falls and AMD/NVDA hold — especially after 3+ down weeks — the recovery is reliable (77%, 73%). The causal mechanism is identical: semis lead the broad market in recoveries because they price in forward earnings and AI/capex cycles ahead of the index.

MSTR DOMINANT (61%) — one anomaly worth watching

When MSTR pumps while SPY falls (DOMINANT chord), the market tends to fall together the following week 61% of the time. MSTR is effectively leveraged BTC exposure in stock form. When it disconnects upward from equity markets, it's often pricing in a crypto move that the broader market hasn't agreed with yet — and that disagreement resolves bearishly. Possible SHORT setup, needs more validation (n=18).

The framework does NOT symmetrically invert

DOMINANT (base up, alt down) does not predict TRITONE with any reliability. Most results cluster at 44–50% — random. This tells us the signal is fundamentally about recovery detection, not crash prediction. Recoveries leave fingerprints (one asset leads early). Crashes are more reflexive and don't show up in divergence patterns beforehand.

Why the avg return is negative for BTC/SOL reversed

When BTC is up and SOL is lagging (DOMINANT), the following week SOL catches up and both go up (MAJOR). So trying to short DOMINANT actually loses money — BTC's upward leadership drags SOL along. This is the transfer entropy direction: BTC leads, SOL follows. DOMINANT is actually a mild delayed-bullish signal for SOL, not bearish.

Laws discovered across all configs

Law 25 — The Hierarchy Law

The SUSPENDED signal only works when the base asset is the dominant market leader with causal primacy. BTC leads crypto. SPY leads equities. Peer pairs (ETH/SOL, SOL/DOT) produce noise or worse. The signal lives in leader → follower relationships, detectable via transfer entropy direction.

Law 26 — The Asymmetry Law

The framework does not invert. Upward divergence (SUSPENDED = alt strong during base weakness) reliably predicts recovery. Downward divergence (DOMINANT = alt weak during base strength) does NOT reliably predict falls. Recoveries are coordinated events with early movers. Crashes are reflexive and don't telegraph via the chord system.

Law 27 — The Beta Amplification Law

Signal quality and return magnitude scale with the alt asset's beta to the base. SOL (highest beta crypto) → 100%, +7.1%. AMD/NVDA (highest beta stocks) → 73-77%, +2.2%. Low-beta alts (AAPL, XLU) → noise. The edge is a volatility amplifier: the same causal structure produces bigger P&L where beta is higher.

Law 28 — The Dying Asset Law

For assets in secular decline (ADA, DOT, XLF as sector), SUSPENDED resolves to TRITONE more often than MAJOR. The SUSPENDED signal assumes the alt asset has recovery potential when the base recovers. Dying assets don't — they continue falling regardless of what BTC or SPY does. The filter should include a trend health check (e.g. price vs 52-week high) before entering.

Law 29 — The MSTR Anomaly

MSTR behaves like a high-beta crypto instrument embedded in equities. Its SUSPENDED signal vs SPY is moderate (57%) but its DOMINANT signal partially inverts (61% TRITONE). When MSTR pumps while SPY falls, it's pricing in a crypto narrative that the equity market rejects within a week. This is the only asset tested where the reversal shows any signal above 60% with meaningful n.
